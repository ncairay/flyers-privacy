刪除帳戶

我們尊重你嘅私隱權利。如果你想刪除你嘅傳單帳戶及所有相關資料，請按照以下步驟操作。

如何提交刪除請求

  1. 發送電郵至以下地址：
    privacy@flyers.app
  2. 喺電郵主題寫上「帳戶刪除請求」
  3. 喺電郵內文提供你註冊時使用嘅電話號碼，以便我們核實你嘅身份

將會刪除嘅資料

帳戶刪除後，以下資料將會被永久移除：

  • 帳戶資料 — 電話號碼、用戶檔案、角色設定
  • 位置資料 — 所有已記錄嘅位置數據
  • 偏好設定 — 傳單類別偏好、通知設定
  • 派發記錄 — 傳單領取及核銷記錄
請注意：匿名化嘅匯總統計數據（例如傳單總瀏覽次數）可能會被保留，但無法追溯至你嘅個人身份。

處理時間

我們會喺收到請求後 30 日內完成帳戶刪除。刪除完成後，你會收到確認電郵。

重要提示：帳戶一旦刪除便無法恢復。如果你日後想再使用傳單，需要重新註冊帳戶。

聯絡我們

如有任何疑問，請聯絡：

電郵：privacy@flyers.app

Delete Account

We respect your privacy rights. If you would like to delete your Flyers account and all associated data, please follow the steps below.

How to Submit a Deletion Request

  1. Send an email to:
    privacy@flyers.app
  2. Use the subject line "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include the phone number you used to register, so we can verify your identity

Data That Will Be Deleted

Upon account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Account Information — Phone number, user profile, role settings
  • Location Data — All recorded location data
  • Preferences — Flyer category preferences, notification settings
  • Delivery History — Flyer collection and redemption records
Please note: Anonymized, aggregated statistics (e.g., total flyer views) may be retained but cannot be traced back to your identity.

Processing Time

We will complete the account deletion within 30 days of receiving your request. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

Important: Account deletion is irreversible. If you wish to use Flyers again in the future, you will need to create a new account.
